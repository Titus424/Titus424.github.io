We Don't Just Sell Fuel
We Deliver At Your Doorstep

This system allows the Users to order petrol or diesel right to their doorstep.
The User simply needs to specify their location and the type of fuel that they require along with the quantity that they need. Users can also make payment online through the system itself. After an order is confirmed, this system relays the order to the gas station.
